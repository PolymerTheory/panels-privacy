# Panels — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

Panels is a comic reader app. Here's what it does with your data:

- **Reading history** is stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to Firebase (Google's cloud) so you can sync across devices. It is never sold or shared with third parties.
- **Anonymous authentication**: if you don't sign in with Google, a random anonymous ID is assigned so your data can be backed up without identifying you.
- **Google Sign-In** (optional): if you sign in, your Google account name is used only to identify your backup. No email is stored or shared.
- **No ads, no tracking, no analytics** beyond crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics).

To delete your data, go to Settings → Delete My Data inside the app.
